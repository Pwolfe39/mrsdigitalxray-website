FUJI DRYPIX 4000

The DryPix 4000 is a dry laser imager which combines the high quality and performance of Fuji's centralized imagers, with an extremely compact design and convenient 110 olt power, required for decentralized imaging applications. The DryPix 4000 has excellent throughput between 110 and 160 films per hour making it ideal for modalities such as high speed CT.

CODONICS HORIZON SF DRY IMAGER

The Horizon SF Multi Media Dry Imager is an intelligent, desktop-sized, grayscale and color output device that produces quality medical hardcopy on a variety of small format media types. The imager accepts many industry standard file formats including Dicom and PostScript and incorporates networking, high speed image processing, networking and spooling capabilities.
