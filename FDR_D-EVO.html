FUJI FDR D-EVO

 
The Fuji FDR D-EVO ™ is a portable flat panel detector (FPD) that introduces an intelligent alternative for analog-to-digital conversions. With quick image availability and short cycle times, FDR D-EVO ™ will improve efficiency and reduce patient waiting without compromising image quality, flexibility or reliability.             REQUEST A QUOTE

 

FUJI FDR D-EVO VIDEO   Fuji FDR D-EVO Brochure

 

 

Currently Analog or CR?

Just plug and play. The Fuji FDR D-EVO ™ easily slides into any existing bucky and can be used for tabletop exams, too. Since the bucky isn't altered, standard cassettes can still be used on demand.

 

Already have Fujifilm's CR?

FDR D-EVO ™'s FDX Console connects to existing FCR and features a user interface consistent with Fujifilm's Flash IIP, making integration and training quick and easy. Additionally, images processed on other FCR devices can be combined seamlessly at FDR D-EVO ™'s FDX Console within one exam screen, further simplifying workflow and maintaining consistency.

 

Already Using DR?

FDR D-EVO ™ is a great complement to any DR room. It's ideal for cross-table lateral exams such as hips and decubitus views. It's also well-suited for unsteady patients who aren't able to stand from their wheelchairs, or stretcher patients who require critical images like AP chest or even extremity exams.

 

FDR D-EVO

The FDR D-EVO has achieved 384 × 460 mm size and thickness of 14 mm which is equivalent to a CR cassette. The other main characteristics of the FDR D-EVO are 2.8 kg* weight, minimum 3seconds preview time and minimum 9seconds cycle time. Since the size is equivalent to a CR cassette, it is possible to load the FDR D-EVO into an existing upright/table X-ray system and handle it in the same fashion as a CR cassette.

 

FDR D-EVO ™ is a smart way to transition to DR without modification to the exam room. FDR D-EVO ™'s size allows it to fit seamlessly into any environment and make an immediate impact. It fits standard cassette holders and bucky devices with no modifications. Thanks to its 14" X 17" design, FDR D-EVO ™ can easily be employed in existing analog x-ray rooms or with integrated DR rooms to add flexibility for any cassette-based clinical needs.

 

FDR D-EVO ™ is Fast With 5 second image previews and 9 second cycle times, FDR D-EVO ™ is perfect for busy environments such as trauma, when quick image availability is critical. Faster image previews means it takes less time to make sure you have the image you need. The result is more efficiency and faster exam completion for your patients. Fujifilm's Outstanding Image Quality As the worldwide leader in digital x-ray, Fujifilm has unparalleled experience in optimizing image quality. FDR D-EVO ™ features 150 micropixel pitch and a wide, 16-bit dynamic range to deliver incredible high-quality images with low noise and outstanding contrast resolution. These technology advances, coupled with Fujifilm's renowned Image Intelligence™ processing, deliver the outstanding image quality you expect from Fujifilm.
